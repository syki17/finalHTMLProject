OUR STORY...



Our story begins back in 1985 in Kingston, Ontario. With the love and passion for lacrosse, and recent College graduates two friends turned their love and passion into a small local start up business. We set up shop in a small 600sqft shop in downtown Kingston. After 5 years we decided it was time to open up more stores to sell our products. We expanded into the GTA area, opening up 10 stores by 2000, and 50 stores by 2015.

Here we are now, with a new and exciting website format, in which we will be able grow our business beyond our borders in which we are confined to with just the typical brick and motar stores.

This is an exciting time for our company..
